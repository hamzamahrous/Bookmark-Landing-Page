Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you’d like answered please feel free to email us.

  • what is bookmark?
    Bookmark is a digital tool that allows you to save web pages for easy access later, helping you organize your favorite sites efficiently.
  • How can i request a new browser?
    To request a new browser support, please email us with your browser details and why you'd like it to be supported.
  • is there a mobile app?
    Currently, we do not have a mobile app, but our website is fully responsive, so you can access it on any mobile device.
  • what about other chromium browsers?
    Our service is compatible with all major Chromium-based browsers, including Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, and Brave.
